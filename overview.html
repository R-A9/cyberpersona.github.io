The Digital World Today

The modern digital world is vast and constantly evolving. Here are some compelling statistics that illustrate the current landscape:

  • As of 2024, there are over 5.3 billion internet users globally

  • Social media platforms have more than 4.6 billion users worldwide

  • The average person spends approximately 6 hours per day online

  • Mobile devices account for over 50% of internet traffic

Tips for Digital Well-being

Set Boundaries

Establishing clear boundaries for device usage can help maintain a healthy balance. For instance, designate specific times of day for checking emails or social media.

Take Breaks

Regular breaks from screens are essential. Follow the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, take a 20-second break and look at something 20 feet away.

Mindful Consumption

Be mindful of the content you consume. Choose quality over quantity and avoid content that negatively impacts your mood or mental health.
