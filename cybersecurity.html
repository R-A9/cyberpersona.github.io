Cybersecurity

Security is a must in the evolving modern world. Data is always critical, and must always be protected. Protect yourself against threats by following the below:

Using an AV Solution

 

What are its benefits?

  • Protection against malicious software
  • Protection against phishing
  • Cost-effective
  • Real-time protection
  • Cloud-based threat detection

VPN

 

What does a VPN do?

  • Protection against public Wi-Fi
  • Prevents IP geolocation by threat actors
  • Encrypts your internet traffic
  • Bypasses censorship
  •  

Password Managers

 

What does a password manager do for me?

  • Strong, complex password generation
  • Protection against keylogging
  • Convenience
  • Password reuse prevention
  • Ease of management

Why should I follow these security practices?

Following at least one of these greatly affects your digital self in a positive way.

Securing your data is a great way to prevent threat actors from using your data and computer in malicious ways. Examples of such would be identity fraud, botting, and more.

What software can I use to lessen the risk?

For password managers, you can use the top 3:

  1. LastPass
  2. NordPass
  3. 1Password
 

For VPN providers, you can use:

  1. OpenVPN
  2. IPVanish
  3. ProtonVPN
 

For AV solutions, you can use:

  1. Kaspersky
  2. Malwarebytes
  3. Avast
   

Using such software will defend you from malicious actors, but all will be for nothing if YOU are not cautious with what you do online.
